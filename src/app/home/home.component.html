Dub+ for Dubtrack.fm

Dub+, also referred to as DubPlus, is Dubtrack.fm's #1 extension and script to enhance your Dubtrack experience!

Dub+

Just drag the Dub+ button to your bookmarks bar, visit any room on Dubtrack.fm, and click your newly created bookmark!

Customize Your Experience

Our easy-to-use menu will help you enjoy your Dubtrack.fm experience more than ever! There are plenty of features to help make Dubtrack even better!

Integrated Menu

Our customization menu is non-obtrusive, and integrates seamlessly into Dubtrack without obstructing your experience. Easy to access and easy to hide.

Please the Crowd

Use our script to keep you notified on when you're DJing next. Get notifications and messages about how well the room is enjoying your songs, so that you can keep queuing songs that will keep the party going!
